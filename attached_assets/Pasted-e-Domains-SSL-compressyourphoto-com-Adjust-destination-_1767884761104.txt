Increased stability through advanced DNS settings
DNS Pro provides reliability and superior performance through a variety of features by distributing DNS data among multiple servers around the world so that visitors to your site are connected to the nearest server location for low latency. DNS Pro is a package of DNS security and management features:

Premium Anycast Low latency and high availability for your domains around the world. (Does not work in combination with secondary DNS)
DNSSEC Prevents hackers from taking control of your website and stealing messages and data. (Does not work in combination with secondary DNS)
DNS templates Create reusable groups of DNS records that can be applied to each of your domains.
DNS backup Back up and restore DNS settings
Secondary DNS Dedicated to DNS administrators/providers who need strong secondary services or want to hide their primary server.
